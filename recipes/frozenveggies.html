Frozen Vegetables!

Description

Vegetables that are frozen.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Veggies
  • Water

Directions

  1. Get a pan and fill it with water about halfway.
  2. Boil water covered.
  3. Once boiling, pour how many veggies you want into pan and cover the pan. Reduce heat.
  4. After ~7 minutes, check the veggies to see if they're soft. If not, let cook another 2 minutes.
  5. Drain water and mix in salt and butter! Enjoy!
